About Us

Our mission is to empower computer science students to discover and pursue fulfilling career paths by providing personalized guidance based on their skills, interests, goals, and personality traits. We aim to create a supportive platform that connects students with valuable resources, insights, and opportunities to make informed decisions about their professional journeys.

Vision

We envision a future where every student has access to tailored guidance and support, enabling them to navigate their unique career journeys with confidence and purpose. By fostering a community of learners and professionals, we aspire to inspire the next generation of innovators and leaders in technology.

Future Iterations

Looking ahead, we plan to enhance our platform through:
Expanded Assessments: Adding more detailed assessments to cover additional skills, interests, and career paths, providing deeper insights for users.
Job Board Integration: Integrating a job board that matches users with job opportunities based on their profiles and preferences.
Resources Integration: Integrating useful resources like courses, workshops classes based on users profiles and preferences.
Other degrees: We would like to expand this to help students from different degrees to find their career paths
